Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple, Pune

The **Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple** is one of the most famous and revered temples in Pune, Maharashtra. The temple is known for its rich history, beautiful Ganpati idol, and grand Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations.

About the Temple

Established over 125 years ago, the temple was built by Dagdusheth Halwai, a famous sweet maker in Pune. The temple attracts thousands of devotees every year, especially during **Ganesh Chaturthi**.

Location & Map

The temple is located in Budhwar Peth, Pune.

Visiting Hours

Monday - Sunday: 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM

How to Reach

The temple is well connected by Pune public transport and is about 3 km from Pune Railway Station.
